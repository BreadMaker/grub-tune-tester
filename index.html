FAQ

How a tune works?

GRUB_INIT_TUNE format:

tempo freq duration [freq duration freq duration...]
  • tempo = base for all note durations in beats per minute (60: 1-second base, 120: half-second base, etc.)
  • freq = frequency (in Hz) of the sound (0: a rest)
  • duration = duration of the sound in a 4/4 bar (1: quarter of a bar, 4: full bar)
