How a tune works

A GRUB_INIT_TUNE uses the following format:

tempo freq duration [freq duration freq duration...]

Where tempo is the base for all note durations (60 gives a 1-second base, 120 gives a half-second base, etc.), freq is the frequency (in hertz) of the sound (set 0 to produce a rest), and duration is the duration of the sound in a 4/4 bar (meaning 1 is a quarter of a bar and 4 a full bar).
